How to Improve the Gloss on Plastic Surfaces如何提高塑料表面的光泽度

January 6, 2026

Achieving the Perfect "Piano Black" Finish实现完美的“钢琴黑”饰面

Achieving a premium "Piano Black" finish requires more than just a polished mold. It involves a deep understanding of pigment dispersion, carrier compatibility, and the use of specialized gloss-enhancing masterbatches. In the competitive world of plastic manufacturing, the surface finish can be the deciding factor for consumers. 实现优质的“钢琴黑”饰面不仅仅需要抛光模具。它涉及对颜料分散、载体相容性以及专用增光色母粒使用的深刻理解。在竞争激烈的塑料制造领域，表面光洁度可能是消费者决策的决定性因素。

Key Factors Influencing Gloss影响光泽度的关键因素

1. Pigment Dispersion: Poor dispersion leads to agglomerates that scatter light, reducing gloss. High-quality masterbatches ensure uniform particle distribution.
2. Carrier Resin Compatibility: The carrier resin of the masterbatch must match the base resin of the product to prevent phase separation, which can cause surface haze.
3. Processing Temperature: Incorrect temperatures can degrade the polymer or additives, leading to surface defects. 1. 颜料分散： 分散不良会导致团聚体散射光线，从而降低光泽度。高质量的色母粒确保颗粒分布均匀。
2. 载体树脂相容性： 色母粒的载体树脂必须与产品的基体树脂匹配，以防止相分离，相分离会导致表面雾影。
3. 加工温度： 不正确的温度会降解聚合物或添加剂，导致表面缺陷。

"Surface aesthetics are not just about appearance; they are a reflection of material quality and processing precision."“表面美学不仅仅关于外观；它是材料质量和加工精度的反映。”
