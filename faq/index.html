Troubleshooting故障诊断

Fast diagnosis checklists and engineering actions for stable production.用工程化步骤快速定位问题并稳定量产。

Quick Answer (ΔE < 1.0):

  • Verify masterbatch heat stability (TDS) vs actual melt temperature.
  • Reduce residence time (barrel utilization too low causes thermal history drift).
  • Lower shear heating (low back pressure + medium screw speed).
  • Use a dedicated carrier (PP for PP, SAN for ABS, etc.).
  • Lock regrind ratio and remove fines/dust.

Expert Reply from Dorothy (SLMMB):

On the injection molding production floor, color variation is often blamed on “unstable masterbatch quality.” In reality, color difference is a multi-variable, non-linear outcome driven by machine, mold, parameters, and the material’s thermal history. To control color variation within ΔE < 1.0, a rheological and thermodynamic diagnosis is required.

1. The Hidden Killer: Thermal History and Residence Time

If barrel capacity is much larger than shot weight (for example, shot weight is only ~15% of barrel capacity), masterbatch experiences excessive residence time and repeated heating cycles. Heat-sensitive pigments (especially organic reds/yellows) may degrade, making parts look dull, darker, or yellowish.

Diagnostic Advice: Check cycle time and barrel utilization. For heat-sensitive pigments, reduce rear-zone temperatures or use better-matched machine sizing to reduce residence time.

2. Back Pressure vs. Shear Heating

Higher back pressure improves mixing, but excessive back pressure increases shear heating. Local temperature spikes (often 20–30°C above setpoints) can damage pigments and create batch-to-batch shade shifts.

SLMMB Approach: Use “low back pressure, medium rotation speed.” Prefer a carrier MFI slightly higher than the base resin to support diffusion under lower shear.

3. Carrier Resin Rheological Mismatch

If you process PP but use a low-cost masterbatch with a PE carrier, melt viscosity and compatibility differences can cause streaks and uneven pigment distribution, appearing as color difference on parts.

Solution: Follow the “Dedicated Carrier” principle: PP carrier for PP products, SAN carrier for ABS products, etc.

4. Regrind Variability

Regrind already has thermal history and tends to be more yellow. Fluctuations in regrind percentage can trigger visible color drift.

Control Strategy: Fix the regrind ratio (e.g., 15%), remove fines/dust, and keep regrind drying/handling consistent.

Dorothy’s Engineering Recommendation

Before tuning machine parameters, check the masterbatch TDS heat resistance. If your process runs at 240°C but the grade is rated for 200°C, parameter tuning cannot solve shade drift.

Need help analyzing a defect? Send “approved parts” and “defective parts” to the SLMMB lab. We can provide spectrophotometric analysis to help pinpoint root causes.

快速结论（目标：ΔE < 1.0）：

  • 先核对色母 TDS 耐热等级是否覆盖实际熔体温度。
  • 降低停留时间（料筒利用率过低会放大热历史漂移）。
  • 控制剪切热（低背压 + 中转速）。
  • 坚持专用载体（PP配PP，ABS配SAN等）。
  • 固定回料比例并去除细粉尘。

Dorothy（SLMMB）的专家回复：

在注塑生产现场，色差常被简单归咎于“色母粒质量不稳定”。实际上，色差是机器、模具、工艺参数以及材料热历史共同作用的多变量非线性结果。要把色差严格控制在 ΔE < 1.0 的范围内，需要从流变学与热力学角度进行系统诊断。

1. 隐形杀手：热历史与停留时间

当料筒容量远大于单次射出重量（例如射出重量仅占料筒容量约15%）时，色母粒会经历更长停留时间与重复受热。对热敏性颜料（尤其有机红/黄）而言，这会导致热降解，表现为发暗、发黑或发黄。

诊断建议：检查周期时间与料筒利用率。对热敏颜料可降低料筒后段温度，并通过更合理的机台匹配来减少停留时间。

2. 背压与剪切热的拉锯

提高背压可以增强混合，但过高背压会显著增加剪切热，局部瞬时温升（常见高出设定 20–30°C）足以破坏颜料结构，触发色差。

SLMMB建议：“低背压 + 中转速”，并优选载体 MFI 略高于基体树脂的色母，便于低剪切条件下自然扩散。

3. 根本原因：载体树脂流变不匹配

PP体系若使用PE载体色母，因相容性与粘度差异，熔体难以形成均相系统，容易出现条纹与不均匀分布，宏观表现为色差。

解决方案：坚持“专用载体”原则：PP产品用PP载体色母，ABS产品用SAN载体色母等。

4. 干扰因素：回料（水口料）

回料已有一次热历史，基色更偏黄。回料比例波动是色偏的高频触发因素。

控制策略：固定回料比例（例如15%），并去除细粉尘，保持回料干燥与投料一致。

Dorothy 的工程建议

在调整机台参数前，请先核对色母 TDS 的耐热等级。如果实际加工温度 240°C，而色母仅耐热 200°C，任何参数微调都难以从根本上解决色差。

需要协助分析缺陷？欢迎寄送“合格件”和“缺陷件”到 SLMMB 实验室，我们可提供分光光度计分析，帮助定位根因。

Composition, carriers, dispersion, and application guidance.组成、载体、分散与应用要点。

Masterbatch is a concentrate of pigments and/or additives pre-dispersed in a carrier resin with processing aids.

Key parameters:

  • Pigment/additive: typically 20–60%
  • Carrier resin: typically 40–80% (depends on application)
  • ΔE / LDR / FPV / MFI / TDS: N/A

Action steps:

  1. Confirm whether your goal is color, function, or both.
  2. Match the carrier resin to your base polymer system.
  3. Validate dosage and dispersion on trial runs.

一句话结论：

色母粒=颜料/助剂在载体中预分散的浓缩母粒，并含必要加工助剂。

关键参数/范围：

  • 颜料/助剂：常见 20–60%
  • 载体树脂：常见 40–80%（随应用而定）
  • ΔE / LDR / FPV / MFI / TDS：不适用

操作步骤：

  1. 明确需求是外观着色、功能改善或两者兼顾。
  2. 选择与基体树脂匹配/相容的载体体系。
  3. 通过试机验证添加比例与分散效果。

One-line answer:

Twin-screw extrusion produces masterbatch by high-shear dispersing pigments/additives into a molten carrier, then filtering and pelletizing.

Key parameters:

  • Key controls: dosing accuracy, temperature profile, screw design, filtration
  • Film/fiber indicator: filter pressure value (melt cleanliness)
  • ΔE / LDR / FPV / MFI / TDS: N/A

Action steps:

  1. Gravimetrically feed pigments/additives and carrier resin into a co-rotating twin-screw extruder.
  2. Use mixing/kneading zones to break agglomerates and fully wet pigments.
  3. Filter the melt, strand-extrude, water-cool, and pelletize.

一句话结论：

双螺杆挤出通过高剪切将颜料/助剂在熔融载体中充分分散，随后过滤、拉条、水冷并切粒成型。

关键参数/范围：

  • 关键控制点：计量精度、温度曲线、螺杆组合、过滤系统
  • 薄膜/纤维常用指标：压滤值（熔体洁净度）
  • ΔE / LDR / FPV / MFI / TDS：不适用

操作步骤：

  1. 计量加入颜料/助剂与载体树脂至同向旋转双螺杆。
  2. 通过混炼/捏合区打散团聚并充分润湿分散。
  3. 熔体过滤后拉条、水冷并切粒。
