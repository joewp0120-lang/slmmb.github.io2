Moisture Absorption & Surface Defect Elimination吸湿与消除表面缺陷

  1. Home

High-Efficiency Moisture Removal高效除湿方案

Defoaming masterbatch, also known as desiccant masterbatch, is specifically formulated to absorb moisture from plastic resins (PE, PP, ABS, HIPS, etc.) during processing. It effectively eliminates "fish eyes," bubbles, and surface streaks caused by moisture, saving you the time and energy of pre-drying.消泡母粒（也称为干燥母粒）专为在加工过程中吸收塑料树脂（PE、PP、ABS、HIPS等）中的水分而配制。它能有效消除由水分引起的“鱼眼”、气泡和表面条纹，为您节省预干燥的时间和能量。

Applications适用工艺

Blown film, extrusion, pipe production, injection molding.吹膜、挤出、管材生产、注塑。

Compatibility适用基材

PP, HDPE, LLDPE, PO, HIPS, etc.
