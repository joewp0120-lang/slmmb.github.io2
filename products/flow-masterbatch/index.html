Artistic Aesthetic Solutions艺术美学方案

Flow masterbatch (also known as marble masterbatch) is used to create unique, natural-looking patterns such as marble, wood grain, and artistic flow lines in plastic products. Perfect for high-end household items, decorative parts, and stationery.流纹母粒（也称为大理石母粒）用于在塑料产品中创造独特、自然的外观图案，如大理石、木纹和艺术流线。非常适合高端家居用品、装饰零件和文具。

Base Resins适用基材

PP, PE, ABS, PS, AS

Key Effects主要效果

Marble, Wood, Flow, Metallic Sparkle.大理石纹、木纹、流纹、金属闪点。
