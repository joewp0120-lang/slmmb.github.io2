Company Profile公司简介

Established in 2015, SLM Masterbatches Co., Ltd is a leading manufacturer of plastic masterbatches located in Dongguan, China. We specialize in the research, development, and production of black, white, color, and functional masterbatches.SLM色母粒有限公司成立于2015年，位于中国东莞，是一家领先的塑料色母粒制造商。我们专注于黑、白、彩及功能性色母粒的研发和生产。

With a 6,000 square meter facility and 6 advanced automated production lines, our annual capacity exceeds 10,000 tons. We are committed to providing high-quality, consistent, and sustainable solutions to customers worldwide.拥有6000平方米的厂房和6条先进的自动化生产线，年产能超过10000吨。我们要致力于为全球客户提供高质量、稳定和可持续的解决方案。

We serve global plastic processors in Mexico, Canada, Brazil, Malaysia, Indonesia, Turkey, Pakistan, and the Philippines, and provide localized certification support for major markets including Mexico (NOM), Brazil (INMETRO), and Indonesia (SNI).我们服务全球塑料加工客户，覆盖墨西哥、加拿大、巴西、马来西亚、印度尼西亚、土耳其、巴基斯坦与菲律宾，并为主要市场提供本地化认证支持，包括墨西哥（NOM）、巴西（INMETRO）与印度尼西亚（SNI）。
